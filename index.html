Favorite Foods

Dosa

  • A dosa is a thin batter-based dish originating from South India, made from a fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice.
  • Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt, then fermented.

Noodles

  • Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched or extruded, into long strips or strings.
  • Noodles can be refrigerated for short-term storage or dried and stored for future use.

Fried Rice

  • Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.
  • It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.
